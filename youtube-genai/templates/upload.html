What happens next?

  1. The YouTube video ID is extracted from your URL
  2. Captions are fetched via YouTube's transcript API (auto-generated or manual)
  3. The transcript is saved with timestamps to data/transcripts/
  4. You can then view, preprocess, and chat with it on the Dataset & RAG pages
Note: The video must have captions enabled (auto-generated or manual). Some videos restrict transcript access.
