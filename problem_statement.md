# Course-Aware Personalized Learning Companion for IIT Madras BS Degree Students

## Problem Statement

Students in the IIT Madras BS Degree program primarily learn through recorded lectures, course notes, PYQs, FAQs, and discussion forums. Since these resources are distributed across multiple platforms, students often spend significant time searching for relevant information, revising concepts, and identifying areas that need improvement. Existing AI tools can answer questions from uploaded content but do not provide course-aware personalization, persistent progress tracking, or integrated learning support.

## Proposed Solution

We propose an AI-powered Course-Aware Learning Companion. The initial prototype will focus on a single IIT Madras BS course, integrating lecture transcripts, course notes, slides, PYQs, FAQs, and discussion forum content into a unified knowledge base.

Using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), the system will provide source-grounded answers with relevant lecture timestamps for quick revision. It will also maintain a learner profile based on quiz performance to identify knowledge gaps, recommend revision topics, generate personalized quizzes, and track learning progress.

An AI Question Intelligence Module will continuously organize PYQs, FAQs, and discussion forum questions by identifying duplicate questions, grouping related concepts, and maintaining an up-to-date repository of common student doubts to improve retrieval quality and personalized learning.

## Key Features

- Course-aware RAG-based Question Answering
- Source-grounded responses with citations
- Lecture Timestamp Navigation
- Personalized Quiz Generation
- Learner Profile & Progress Tracking
- Knowledge Gap Detection & Revision Recommendations
- AI Question Intelligence Module
- Learning Analytics Dashboard

- Quiz Performance & Interaction History
